What is The Lighthouse?

The Lighthouse is an organization on a mission to bring hope back to the community of Bunker Hill Indiana by investing in young people. We have weekly gatherings where kids in grades 6-12 eat, play, and learn together. We are like a youth group, but we're not associated with a specific church. We have outgrown our current location and therefore have had to ask kids to stop inviting their friends. We are now in the construction phase of a new building on a 3-acre property we purchased in Bunker Hill that was the old Bunker Hill grain elevator. The new building will allow us to reach out to more young people at more times throughout the week.

A Lighthouse gathering is like a family reunion where everybody wants to be together. We provide a full home-cooked meal every week. We play all kinds of games, indoor and outdoor. Weather permitting, we play a lot of ultimate frisbee and volleyball. The gatherings also include a group discussion or lesson, as well as lots of encouraging music.

When and Where Are the Meetings?

The Lighthouse gatherings currently take place every Wednesday from 6p - 9p.

We meet at the home of Chris and Yvonne Edgington, 1304 W 800 S, right outside of Bunker Hill, next to the Nickel Plate Trail.
